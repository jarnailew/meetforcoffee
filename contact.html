Our pick this month.

KAHAILA, Brick Lane

Kahaila regards itself as a coffee shop with a conscience, so they make sure that all their products are ethically sourced and where possible, locally sourced. As a charity, any profit that is made there goes into supporting local community projects and other charitable causes which makes the coffee taste even better!
