What do we do?

Are you planning to hang out with your friends but sick of going to Starbucks or Costa Coffee every time you want a catch up?
We know the feeling. Let us help!

meet for coffee was set up by three coffee and coding aficionados to help Londoners find somewhere new to grab a latte. London is such a huge, bustling city and coffee culture is booming - there's never been a better time to get involved!

So, the next time you're about to send off a meet for coffee? text to your group chat, visit our website and open your eyes to the great places to hang out in our beautiful city.
